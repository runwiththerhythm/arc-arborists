Careers

Why Work With Us?

  • Expert Team: Work alongside highly skilled arborists who are leaders in their field.
  • Career Growth: We offer continuous training and development to help you achieve your career goals.
  • Sustainability Focus: Contribute to projects that prioritise environmental stewardship.
  • Supportive Environment: Be part of a close-knit, collaborative team that values safety, quality, and respect.
  • Flexible Working Schedule: Whether part-time or full-time, we have shifts to fit around your life's priorities.
  • Gym Membership & Health Plans: Tree work requires fitness, and we care about your health.

Current Vacancies

Arborist / Climber

Hours: Full-time

Salary: Competitive, based on experience

Key Responsibilities:

  • Performing tree pruning, felling, and other arboricultural tasks.
  • Using climbing equipment and machinery safely and efficiently.
  • Ensuring compliance with health and safety standards.

Requirements:

  • Previous climbing experience and relevant certifications (e.g., NPTC, Lantra).
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills.
  • A passion for arboriculture and commitment to quality.

Grounds Crew Member

Hours: Full-time & part-time

Salary: Competitive

Key Responsibilities:

  • Supporting arborists with ground-based tasks, including equipment setup and site cleanup.
  • Operating wood chippers and other machinery.
  • Ensuring work sites are safe and tidy.

Requirements:

  • A positive attitude and willingness to learn.
  • Experience in landscaping or arboriculture (preferred but not essential).

Office Administrator

Hours: Part-time

Salary: Competitive

Key Responsibilities:

  • Coordinating schedules, client bookings, and communication.
  • Managing invoicing and payroll processes.
  • Providing general administrative support to the team.

Requirements:

  • Strong organisational and communication skills.
  • Proficiency in office software (e.g., Microsoft Office, CRM systems).
  • Experience in a similar role (preferred).

Apprenticeships and Training Programs

Apprenticeships

Our structured apprenticeship program is designed for individuals passionate about starting a career in arboriculture.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Tree care techniques, including pruning, planting, and removal.
  • Safe operation of climbing equipment and machinery.
  • Environmental best practices and sustainability in arboriculture.

Who Should Apply?

  • School leavers, career changers, or anyone eager to learn.
  • Enthusiastic, physically fit individuals with a love for the outdoors.

How to Apply: Send your CV and a short cover letter to careers@arc-arborists.uk. Applications are accepted year-round.

Training Courses

We offer professional training courses for those seeking to enhance their skills or gain industry certifications.

Courses Available:

  • Basic Climbing and Aerial Rescue (NPTC Level 2).
  • Tree Inspection and Risk Assessment.
  • Chainsaw Maintenance and Cross-Cutting.
  • Specialist courses tailored to your goals (e.g., cabling, bracing, or ecological assessments).

How to Apply

If you’re interested in joining our team, please send your CV and a cover letter to careers@arc-arborists.uk specifying the role you’re applying for.

Don’t see the role for you? We’re always on the lookout for talented individuals. Feel free to send us your CV for future opportunities.
